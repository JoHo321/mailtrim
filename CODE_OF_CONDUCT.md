# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be direct, kind, and constructive
- Assume good faith
- Welcome contributors of all experience levels
- Focus on the technical problem, not the person

## Not Acceptable

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Dismissing questions because they seem "basic"
- Sharing others' personal information without consent

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and any public communication where you represent this project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainer. Repeated or severe violations will result in a ban from the project.
